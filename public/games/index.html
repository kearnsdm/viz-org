8-bit NES-style games. Keyboard recommended — click the game once, then play.
Brooklyn Run NEWPLAY ▶
Navigate a route across Brooklyn by reading a mini-map and turning at each corner. Forward legs play the top-down delivery game; left/right legs are jump-over side-scrollers. Brick brownstones, time-of-day shifts that pay more (and pack the streets), and a graded difficulty ramp.
Meals On Wheel PLAY ▶
The original: a Paperboy-style top-down delivery run. Sling food to glowing customers, dodge cars and dogs, build combos and tips, and survive the rush-hour boss every third street.
Controls: Z / X toss · ↑ / Space jump · arrows steer · Enter start · P pause · M mute  ·  viz-org
